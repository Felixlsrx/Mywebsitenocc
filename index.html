my favorite keyboards

A short showcase of three Redragon boards I like.

  • Redragon K631 Pro SE — 65%
    Compact layout that keeps arrows and a few nav keys. Great for small desks and portability.

    More from Redragon

  • Redragon K686 Pro SE — 98 keys
    Large layout with near-full functionality in a compact footprint — numeric pad included.
  • Redragon K530 Draconic
    Affordable 60% board, good for gaming and tight desk setups.

Notes

These three keyboards are listed in preference of layout and portability.
